The workshop will feature keynote talks, technical paper presentations, a panel discussion on the future of mixed reality adoption in healthcare, and hands-on demonstrations of MR systems and prototype workflows. A detailed schedule will be announced soon.

## Preliminary Programme

- Theme 1: Interventional Oncology and Augmented Reality
- Theme 2: Preoperative Planning and Clinical Training using MR
- Panel: Future of Mixed Reality Adoption in Healthcare
- Demo session with coffee break and poster presentation

## Planned Session Types

- Keynote talks by leading experts in MR and surgical AI
- Technical sessions for peer-reviewed papers and clinical case studies
- Panel discussion with academic, clinical, and industry perspectives
- Hands-on demonstrations of MR systems and workflows
